About Fujaefilm

Welcome to Robert Jaime "RJ" D.C. Erandio's FUJAEFILM, a captivating travel photography blog that takes you on a visual odyssey around the world. Through stunning imagery and compelling narratives, RJ captures the essence of remarkable destinations, showcasing their beauty and cultural richness. Join us on this extraordinary adventure as we explore hidden gems, iconic landmarks, and diverse cultures, inviting you to experience the awe-inspiring wonders of our planet.

We can checkout his recent works and experiences below. He considers photography as his passion more than profession.

what am i doing?

  • I embark on captivating journeys to various corners of the globe, capturing the beauty of landscapes, the vibrancy of cities, and the richness of cultures. Join me as I delve into unique experiences and share stunning visuals that transport you to these incredible destinations.
  • Beyond the photographs, I share the captivating stories behind each image. I believe that every place has a tale to tell, and I aim to bring these narratives to light through my blog posts. Discover the hidden gems, cultural traditions, and fascinating encounters that shape my travel experiences.
  • Photography is a shared passion, and I believe in building a community of like-minded individuals. Join the Fujaefilm community through comments, discussions, and social media engagement. Connect with fellow photographers, share your own experiences, and find inspiration from others who share the same love for capturing the beauty of the world.
  • Fujaefilm is not just a photography website; it's a visual journey that invites you to explore, learn, and be inspired. So, come along and immerse yourself in the wonders of travel photography. Let's capture the world together and make memories that will last a lifetime.
